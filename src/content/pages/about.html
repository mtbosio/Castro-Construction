Meet Our Management

Michael started Castro Construction in 1998 building arbors, fences, and decks. With the high quality of his work, he soon found himself managing remodels and larger projects. Michael grew his company and started framing large, estate-style homes in Pebble Beach and Los Gatos. His main focus is quality construction, customer service and budget. In his spare time, he enjoys surfing, hiking, traveling, and spending time with his three daughters.
